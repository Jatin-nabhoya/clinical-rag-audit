Analysis & Final Report
reports/phase8/ · generate_analysis.py · final_report.md
✅ Complete
Three Validated Findings
1
Mistral-7B achieves best overall correctness (52.7%)
Most balanced refusal calibration — refuses 27/29 unanswerable while answering 22/30 answerable. Highest ROUGE-L (0.160 [0.137–0.187]).
2
Over-refusal is the dominant failure mode (35–55%), not fabrication (≤1.8%)
Safety-tuned open-source LLMs in clinical RAG err toward excessive caution. Llama-3's over-refusals are retriever-driven (wrong chunks retrieved) — correct remediation is retriever improvement, not prompt changes.
3
Phi-3-mini ignores retrieved context (overlap 0.199 vs ~0.50 for others)
Phi-3 uses parametric knowledge to complete partial answers without flagging the gap. Combined with 10.0% gap-filling and 7.3% factual drift — the most unreliable model for clinical deployment.

Known Limitation (§5)
No RAG-vs-no-RAG Ablation
Not executed due to GPU time constraints. We cannot claim “RAG reduces hallucination” — only that these three models under RAG exhibit the measured patterns. Llama-3’s over-refusal is retriever-driven; correct remediation is retriever improvement, not prompt changes.
